QUANTUM DEFENDER GUIDE

OBJECTIVE

Protect the reactor core from quantum anomalies. Each anomaly that reaches the core drains your energy. Eliminate all anomalies before time runs out.

CONTROLS

CLICK on anomalies to eliminate them. CLICK AND DRAG defense nodes to connect them and create barriers.

ABILITIES

SLOW TIME: Slows anomalies for 5 seconds.
ENERGY SHIELD: Protects the core for 8 seconds.
QUANTUM BLAST: Destroys all visible anomalies.

SCORING

+10 points per anomaly destroyed. +50 for completing a level. Bonus points for remaining energy and time.
